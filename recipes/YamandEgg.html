Nigerian Yam and Egg

Description

NIgerian Yam and Egg is one of the best food combo to be made,it is very soothing to the soul make this for a Nigerian man or woman and you will have access to his or her heart

Ingredients

  • Yam (white yam)
  • Add salt for it to taste good and you can also add sugar which is optional
  • the quantity of water you are gona add to it depends on the yam
  • groundnut oil which is use to fry the egg

Steps to follow when making yam and egg

  1. Peel and cut the yam into tiny circles, wash the cut yams and place in a bowl
  2. Add salt to the content of the bowl and mix very well
  3. Heat up vegetable oil in a pot, enough to deep fry the yam chips
  4. When the oil is heated up, add the yam chips batch by batch to create enough room for flipping of the content of the pot.
  5. When they have turn golden, remove and place in a paper towel to absorb the oil
  6. Serve with fried egg, omelette, tomato or vegetable sauce.
  7. Bon Appetit
