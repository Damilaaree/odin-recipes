Nigerian Jollof Rice

Description

Nigerian Jollof rice is a RICH comforting meal made from rice cooked in a flavor-packed tomato based sauce. For an extra depth of flavor, authentic party jollof is cooked with fire wood which infuses a smokey flavor into the dish and its simply magical,try this jollof rice and your life will never remain the same.

Ingredients

  • 5 medium tomatos
  • 3 medium bell peppers
  • 2 scotch bonnet pepper
  • 1 medium onion
  • Â½ cup vegetable oil or oil of choice
  • 2 knorr seasoning cubes or 2 teaspoons crushed bullion cube
  • thyme and curry powder
  • 4 cups of water
  • 8 cups of rice

Steps to follow for making a delicious Nigerian Jollof Rice

  1. Transfer pepper mix to a wide pot and boil until reduce(about 15 minutes depending on how much water was used to blend peppers
  2. Wash rice multiple times until water runs clear
  3. In a large wide pot, heat up vegetables oil or oil of choice
  4. Fry chopped onion until onion is translucent and fragrant.Add tin tomato/tomato paste(different from tomato sauce). Fry tomato paste for about 15 minutes until it looks grainy. (Frying the tomato paste gets rid of the tangy taste)
  5. Add boiled down pepper mix,season with salt, curry, thyme, bay leaves. fry for an additional 10 minutes.
  6. Add washed rice, cover with foil and allow to cook for about 20 minutes
  7. After 20 minutes your rice should be ready. If you want the authentic part jollof taste cook on high heat for the first 25 minutes, reduce heat and let rice absolve the Smokey flavor generated from the Burt to rice
  8. Open foil, turn rice to release steam. If rice seems dry, add 3 teaspoon of butter, allow to melt with the heat.
  9. Serve with chicken, fried plantain and salad.
